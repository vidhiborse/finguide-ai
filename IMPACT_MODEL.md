# FinGuide AI — Business Impact Model

## Target Market

| Segment | Size |
|---------|------|
| Demat account holders in India | 14 crore (SEBI 2024) |
| Indians with a formal financial plan | ~8% (AMFI survey) |
| **Target: salaried urban Indians, 25-45, with at least 1 SIP** | **3 crore users** |

---

## Value Delivered Per User Per Year

### 1. Tax Savings — ₹18,000/user/year
- Average salaried user in 20% bracket leaves ₹90,000 of 80C unused
- FinGuide AI identifies this → ₹90,000 × 20% = **₹18,000 saved**
- Additional NPS 80CCD(1B) benefit: ₹50,000 × 20% = ₹10,000 more
- **Assumption**: User acts on at least 1 recommendation

### 2. Expense Ratio Savings — ₹9,400/user/year
- Average user has ₹8L AUM in active funds at 1.2% TER
- FinGuide X-Ray identifies → switches to Nifty50 index at 0.05% TER
- Saving: ₹8L × (1.2% - 0.05%) = **₹9,200 saved annually**
- **Assumption**: User moves 50% of AUM to index funds

### 3. Financial Advisor Fee Replaced — ₹25,000/user/year
- Minimum CFP fee in India: ₹25,000/year
- FinGuide AI delivers equivalent analysis in under 5 minutes
- **Value**: ₹25,000 in advisor fees saved

### 4. Behavioural Value (Avoiding Panic Sell) — ₹35,000/user/year
- Average Indian retail investor panic-sold in March 2020 correction
- XIRR impact of one bad exit: -3% to -5% on ₹7L portfolio = ₹35,000
- FinGuide AI shows real XIRR vs assumed → reduces panic selling
- **Assumption**: 1 market event per year where AI guidance helps

## Assumptions Summary

1. Target pool of 3 crore = salaried, urban, 25-45, at least 1 active SIP
2. Average AUM of ₹8 lakh per user (conservative — AMFI data shows avg SIP size ₹2,200/month for 5+ years)
3. Tax bracket assumed 20% (₹8-12L income range)
4. At least 50% of users act on one recommendation per year
5. One market correction event per year where behavioural guidance adds value
6. Advisor fee comparison based on SEBI-registered RIA minimum fee

---

*These are conservative mid-range estimates. Best-case scenario (30% market penetration) delivers over ₹78,000 crore in annual value to Indian retail investors.*
